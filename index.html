Hello There, I’m Samshitha.

I’m 20 and passionate about designing and coding things.

When I was around 11 years old, I wanted to make games which looked like those in the Yahoo games website. However, those thoughts I had in me faded with time. I first got into coding when I was 14. Since then, I loved coding and chose paths that would make me a good coder. I had to give up coding during my +1 and +2 studies, but my passion for coding never faded. So I took up Computer Science as my major at University. However, things did not go as planned, and I could not still find what I wanted to do or how to apply my code.


It was until 2019 that I realised I wanted to design and code apps/websites.

I know, much time got wasted. There were times where I was lazy; there were times where I was motivated, times where I could not find ways. No matter how the rollercoaster went, I realised that anything that my mind focused on those times, consciously or unconsciously, must be manifested.
